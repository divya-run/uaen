Being an Emirati frontliner is one of the most noble things you can do for your country and humanity. You can ensure a better future for your family and country. The scariest part during my Covid-19 experience was the uncertainty during the beginning of the pandemic, but seeing all the efforts being put in place by our wise leadership, made us more confident and secure, knowing that we have a solid backbone to lean on.
As a frontliner, you feel that this is your moment of pride, and you will never regret any effort or time you offer. The huge appreciation from the whole world gave us the strength and power needed to continue, especially during the early beginnings. Some people may see us as heroes, but I consider my personal hero in life as Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan, who believed in this country and its huge potential.
